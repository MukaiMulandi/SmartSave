Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SmartSave and personal finance.

How does SmartSave keep my financial data secure?

We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and never store your banking credentials. Our read-only access means no one can move money from your accounts. We are regularly audited for security.

Is there a fee to use SmartSave?

SmartSave offers a 30 day free basic plan with core features. Our premium plan ($20/month) includes advanced analytics, personalized investment advice, and priority support.

What banks and financial institutions do you support?

We support over 10,000 financial institutions in Kenya including all major banks, debit and credit card companies. If your institution isn't listed, contact us and we'll work to add it.

How does the AI provide personalized recommendations?

Our AI analyzes your income, spending patterns, debts, and goals to create custom suggestions. It learns from millions of anonymized financial profiles to identify opportunities you might miss.

Can I use SmartSave if I'm new to personal finance?

Absolutely! SmartSave is designed specifically for young adults and beginners. We explain financial concepts in simple terms and provide educational resources to build your money skills.
